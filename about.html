About Us

Learn More About Us

Vimal Masale are prepared using only fresh and Selected spices, they are guaranteed not only to be 100% pure, but also delight your palate with their taste and aroma.

  • Salaria Food & Spices is a family-owned business that has been producing a wide range of spice blends and food products for over four decades.
  • The brand is known for its commitment to quality and its use of high-quality ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers.

Vimal Masale offers a variety of spice blends that cater to different tastes and preferences, such as garam masala, chaat masala, biryani masala, pav bhaji masala, and more. The brand's products are known for their high-quality ingredients and authentic taste, and are widely used in Indian households and restaurants.
